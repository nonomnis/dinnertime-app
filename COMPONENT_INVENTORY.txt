DESIGN SYSTEM SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

COLOR PALETTE:
- Primary: Tomato Red (#F44336) + tints/shades
- Secondary: Herb Green (#4CAF50) + tints/shades
- Accent: Butter Yellow (#FFEB3B) + tints/shades
- Warm: Golden yellows for accents
- Neutral: Warm grays for text/backgrounds

MEAL CATEGORIES:
- Breakfast: bg-yellow-100, text-yellow-800
- Lunch: bg-blue-100, text-blue-800
- Dinner: bg-purple-100, text-purple-800
- Snack: bg-orange-100, text-orange-800

SPACING:
- Base unit: 4px (Tailwind spacing)
- Component padding: 16px (p-4)
- Section gaps: 16px (gap-4)
- Touch targets: Minimum 44px × 44px

BORDER RADIUS:
- Buttons/inputs: 8px (rounded-lg)
- Cards: 12px (rounded-xl)
- Avatars: 50% (rounded-full)

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Font family: Inter (system-ui fallback)
- Headings: Bold weight
- Body: Regular weight
- Labels: Medium weight

ANIMATIONS:
- Library: Framer Motion v11.0.0
- Spring physics for natural feel
- Tap feedback (scale: 0.95-1)
- Modal entrance: slide up + fade
- Duration: 200-300ms

RESPONSIVE BREAKPOINTS:
- Mobile: Default (320px+)
- Tablet: md (768px+)
- Desktop: lg (1024px+)

SAFE AREA SUPPORT:
- iOS notch: safe-area-inset-*
- Bottom nav: pb-safe-bottom
- Header: safe-top offset
